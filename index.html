More About Me

“Numbers have an important story to tell. They rely on you to give them a clear and convincing voice.” – Stephen Few


This above quote is what I strongly believe in and aim to achieve a level of capability as an Analyst where I can weave a convincing story backed with strong data facts to help businesses tap into measurable insights. I believe that businesses nowadays need a smart and astute thinker, thus by putting in hours of efforts on analyzing varied Datasets I have built on quick and critical thinking developing a knack for asking the right questions.

During my Master’s program I have become aware about the importance of Business Intelligence towards Information Management. I have worked on skills to discover inefficient business process and hidden patterns, identify new opportunities and build on existing strengths. My idea behind developing a combination of skills around data and business intelligence has been to help organizations plan ahead of time for expected upturns or downturns. Presenting your case is as important as unravelling a fact. Thus with my skills on visualization tools like Tableau and Power BI, I believe in building meaningful visualizations to help convince Executives.

I am also a statistics enthusiast. As we know statistics forms the under path for science which is enabling develop unthinkable predictive models in various industries. I aspire to build my skillset on this side of the game called “Data Boom!”.

My undergraduate is in Electrical Engineering from VJTI, Mumbai, India. On completion of under graduation, I joined BA Continuum India Pvt. Ltd. as a software developer in the Risk Technology team. This role presented me with a plethora of opportunities to develop my technical skills and also it initiated my passion for the subject of Finance. The experiences here made me realize that functional knowledge coupled with technical knowledge are crucial for one’s growth as a professional. To ensure this multi-faceted growth and to fulfill my dream of leading an organization to solve complex business problems using technical solutions, I am set on this path of becoming a clear and convincing voice!!
